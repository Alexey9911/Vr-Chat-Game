# Audio Files for Skin-Specific Music

Place your MP3 audio files in this folder with the following names:

## Required Files:

- `elon-meme.mp3` - Audio for Elon skin
- `trumpdraco-meme.mp3` - Audio for Trump Draco skin
- `ai16z-meme.mp3` - Audio for AI16Z skin
- `adminskin-meme.mp3` - Audio for Admin skin

## File Requirements:

- **Format:** MP3 (recommended for best browser compatibility)
- **Volume:** Will be played at 50% volume (0.5)
- **Behavior:** Plays once when triggered, can be triggered multiple times
- **No audio for:** Default soldier skin

## How it Works:

1. Player presses **M** key or clicks the **🔊 button** (top-right)
2. Audio file for their current skin is broadcasted to all players in the lobby
3. Everyone in the lobby hears the audio at 50% volume
4. Audio plays once and stops

## Future Enhancement (Phase 4):

Spatial audio with volume based on distance between players will be added later.
